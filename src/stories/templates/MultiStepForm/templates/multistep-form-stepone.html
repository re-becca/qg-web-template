Consent

Step 1 of 7

Required fields are marked with an *

By using this service

  • I confirm that the child was born in Queensland.
  • I confirm the birth of this child has not been registered before (online or in person).
  • I consent to the Registry of Births, Deaths and Marriages (RBDM) using my email address and mobile number for the purpose of providing me with an electronic copy of the information provided and for contact using SMS and email that relates to this service.
  • I understand that it is my responsibility to ensure that I have nominated a secure email address for RBDM to send the completed application and any related correspondence to.
  • I acknowledge that it is my responsibility to ensure the security of that information upon receipt of it.

  1. * By checking this box, you agree that you have read and understood all of the information above.
