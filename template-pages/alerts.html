Emergency contacts

In an emergency

For police, fire and ambulance:

  • Call Triple Zero (000)
  • TTY: 106

For urgent assistance during a flood, storm, fire or landslide

Call the State Emergency Service (SES) on 132 500

Other help

  • 13 HEALTH: 13 43 25 84
  • Emergency housing assistance:
    13 QGOV (13 74 68)

Watch for the warnings

The new Australian Warning System (AWS) means every warning has an icon and colour that tells you what the hazard is and what the danger level is. Each level has a set of action statements to give you clear advice about what to do.
